# Brand Visual Direction

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name:** Modalin
- **Tagline (ID):** Hitung modal, atur harga, jaga untung.
- **Tagline (EN):** Calculate cost, set prices, protect profit.

## Brand Personality
- Helpful
- Warm
- Practical
- Encouraging
- Clear
- Trustworthy
- Friendly
- Modern
- UMKM-oriented

## Visual Mood
- **Warm Neutral Background:** Evokes comfort and approachability.
- **Orange/Amber Energy:** Reflects the food & beverage industry, warmth, and action.
- **Green Success/Profit:** Represents financial health and positive outcomes.
- **Rounded Cards:** Softens the data, making forms feel like friendly dialogue rather than rigid ledgers.
- **Clean Dashboard:** Focuses on the few numbers that matter.
- **Calm Result Screen:** Even if the margin is low, the UI guides the user peacefully toward a solution.
- **Clear Status Badges:** Instantly communicates financial health without making the user do mental math.

## User Emotional Goal
- **What the UI should make users feel:** Relieved, confident, capable, supported, and professional.
- **What to avoid:** Intimidated, confused, overwhelmed, judged for bad margins.

## Brand Keywords
Accessible, Appetizing, Reassuring, Structured, Vibrant.

## Avoid
- Dark-heavy fintech style (no black glossy backgrounds).
- Corporate banking stiffness (no rigid sharp corners and thin gray lines).
- Too many charts in MVP (no complex data visualizations yet).
- Neon colors (keep it warm and earthy).
- Overly playful cartoonish style (it still needs to be trusted with money).
- Dense spreadsheet layout (no dense tables on mobile).
- Harsh red warnings unless truly dangerous (e.g., selling at a heavy loss).
