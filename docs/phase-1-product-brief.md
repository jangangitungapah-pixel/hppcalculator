# Phase 1 Product Brief

## Product Name Placeholder
**HPP Cepat** (working title) - *Friendly F&B Cost Calculator*

## Product Summary
A mobile-first, beginner-friendly web application designed to help Indonesian home-based F&B (Food and Beverage) businesses calculate their production costs (HPP), determine selling prices, and analyze profit margins without needing accounting knowledge.

## Vision
To become the most trusted and user-friendly digital financial companion for micro and small F&B businesses in Southeast Asia.

## Mission
To empower small F&B business owners with simple, accessible tools to understand their costs, price their products profitably, and grow their businesses confidently.

## Main Problem
Many home-based F&B sellers struggle to accurately calculate their production costs. They often guess their selling prices, which leads to unknown profit margins and, ultimately, business losses. Existing accounting apps and spreadsheets are too complex and intimidating for non-technical users.

## Target Users
Indonesian home-based F&B UMKM (Usaha Mikro, Kecil, dan Menengah). This includes small food sellers, home bakeries, snack makers, frozen food producers, and small beverage stalls.

## Core Value Proposition
A friendly pricing assistant that instantly calculates production cost, HPP, profit, and margin, while providing simple and actionable pricing recommendations.

## SaaS Direction
The product will start as a free, local-storage-only Progressive Web App (PWA). Over time, it will evolve into a full SaaS platform offering cloud sync, advanced reporting, AI-driven business insights, recipe management, and premium subscriptions for growing businesses.

## MVP Boundary
The Phase 1 Minimum Viable Product (MVP) is strictly limited to:
- A Quick HPP Calculator for instant, simple calculations.
- Local data persistence (using browser `localStorage`).
- Bilingual UI (Indonesian as primary, English secondary).
- No backend, no login, no cloud database.

## Non-Goals for MVP
- User authentication and cloud sync.
- Complex recipe costing with multi-layered ingredients.
- Payment gateways or billing systems.
- Native mobile app deployment (iOS/Android App Stores).
