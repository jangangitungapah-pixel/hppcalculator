# UX Writing Guidelines

## Tone of Voice
- **Friendly & Approachable:** Like a helpful business partner or mentor.
- **Encouraging:** Celebrate good margins, gently warn about losses without inducing panic.
- **Simple & Practical:** No complex accounting jargon. Keep sentences short.
- **Professional but Warm:** Build trust through clarity and empathy for UMKM struggles.

## Indonesian Copywriting Style
- Use casual but polite Indonesian (Bahasa gaul yang sopan).
- Pronouns: Use "Kamu" (You) instead of "Anda". Use "Kami" (We) for the app.
- Salutations: "Halo, Juragan!" or "Halo, Kak!".
- Focus on familiar market terms: "Modal", "Untung", "Harga Jual".

## English Copywriting Style
- Clear, conversational, and direct.
- Pronouns: "You" and "We".
- Use standard business terms but keep them accessible (e.g., "Production Cost" instead of "Cost of Goods Sold").

## Words to Use vs. Avoid

| Avoid (Intimidating/Jargon) | Use Instead (Indonesian) | Use Instead (English) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| COGS / Harga Pokok Penjualan | HPP / Modal per Produk | Cost per Unit / HPP |
| Overhead / Depresiasi | Biaya Tambahan (Gas, Listrik) | Additional Costs (Gas, etc.) |
| Invalid Input / Error | Cek lagi angkanya ya | Please check your numbers |
| Revenue | Total Hasil Jual | Total Revenue |
| Submit | Hitung Sekarang | Calculate Now |

## Messaging Examples

### Tooltips
- **Biaya Tambahan:** "Masukkan biaya lain seperti gas, listrik, atau ongkos kirim bahan."
- **Additional Cost:** "Enter other costs like gas, electricity, or delivery fees."

### Error Messages
- **Bad:** "Input COGS value is invalid."
- **Good (ID):** "Biaya produksi belum lengkap. Coba cek lagi angka yang kamu masukkan."
- **Good (EN):** "Production costs are incomplete. Please double-check your inputs."

### Empty States
- **Saved Items (ID):** "Belum ada perhitungan yang disimpan. Yuk, mulai hitung HPP produk pertamamu!"
- **Saved Items (EN):** "No saved calculations yet. Let's calculate your first product's cost!"

### Success Messages
- **Save (ID):** "Sip! Perhitungan berhasil disimpan."
- **Save (EN):** "Awesome! Calculation saved successfully."

### Recommendation Messages
- **Healthy Margin (ID):** "Mantap! Margin keuntungan kamu 40%. Bisnismu sangat sehat dan punya ruang untuk diskon promo."
- **Loss Warning (ID):** "Wah, harga jualmu di bawah modal (Rugi Rp2.000/produk). Coba naikkan harga jual atau kurangi biaya bahan."
