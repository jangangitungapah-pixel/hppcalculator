# Calculation Formulas

### 1. Total Production Cost (Total Modal Produksi)
`Sum of all individual cost items`
The total amount of money spent to produce a given batch.

### 2. Sellable Quantity (Jumlah Hasil Jual)
`Output Quantity - Failed Quantity`
The actual number of successful products that can be sold.

### 3. HPP per Unit (Harga Pokok Penjualan)
`Total Production Cost / Sellable Quantity`
The base cost required to manufacture a single sellable unit.

### 4. Gross Revenue (Total Pendapatan Kotor)
`Selling Price * Sellable Quantity`
Total revenue generated if all sellable units are sold.

### 5. Profit per Unit (Untung per Produk)
`Selling Price - HPP per Unit`
The gross profit obtained from a single item.

### 6. Total Profit (Total Keuntungan)
`Profit per Unit * Sellable Quantity` (or `Gross Revenue - Total Production Cost`)
The overall money made after subtracting costs.

### 7. Margin Percent
`(Profit per Unit / Selling Price) * 100`
Measures how much of the selling price is actual profit. A 40% margin means Rp4,000 out of a Rp10,000 item is pure profit.

### 8. Markup Percent
`(Profit per Unit / HPP per Unit) * 100`
Measures how much the price was raised above the cost.

### 9. Suggested Selling Price
`HPP per Unit / (1 - Target Margin Percent)`
Used to recommend safe prices for specific target margins (e.g., 25%, 40%, 55%).

### 10. Profit Status
Based on `Margin Percent`:
- **Loss:** Margin < 0%
- **Low:** Margin 0% - 15%
- **Okay:** Margin 15% - 30%
- **Good:** Margin 30% - 50%
- **Excellent:** Margin >= 50%
